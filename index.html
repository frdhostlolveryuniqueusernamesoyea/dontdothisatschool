About

This website is a collection of commands that you should not run on your school's computer or on your friends' computer.

The commands are harmless and will not cause any damage to the computer, but they will make the computer do things that the owner might not appreciate.

The commands are meant for educational purposes only and should not be used to harm or annoy anyone.

If you have any suggestions for commands that should be added to the website, please let me know. You can find my contact information on my website.
